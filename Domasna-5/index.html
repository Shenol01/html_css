GALATASARAY CLAIM TURKISH TITLE FOR THE 25TH TIME

Five-star glory for the Lions as they write Turkish football history.

Galatasaray hosted Kayserispor in Matchweek 34 of the Turkish Süper Lig and secured a historic 25th league title with a commanding 3-0 victory at RAMS Park.

With this triumph, Galatasaray become the first and only Turkish club to earn the right to wear a fifth star above their crest – each star representing five domestic league championships. The Lions have now extended their record as the most successful club in Turkish top-flight history.

Goals: Osimhen, Yilmaz, Muslera .Venue: RAMS Park, Istanbul

Galatasaray, led by Head Coach Okan Buruk, have now won the title three times in a row. The team also boasts the best offensive and defensive records in the league this season.

Galatasaray 1-0 Liverpool

Galatasaray hosted Liverpool in the UEFA Champions League Matchday 2 fixture at the Ali Sami Yen Sports Complex RAMS Park and claimed a 1-0 victory.

Liverpool, who mainly attacked down the right flank, were met by our solid defensive line. In the 14th minute, the visitors had a dangerous chance through Ekitike, but Davinson Sánchez cleared the ball off the line. During the ensuing scramble, our defense held firm and neutralized the threat.

Moments later, Barış Alper Yılmaz once again challenged the opposing goal. After dribbling past Szoboszlai in a one-on-one situation, he was brought down in the box, and the referee pointed to the spot. Victor Osimhen calmly sent the goalkeeper the wrong way in the 16th minute to make it 1–0.

Despite eight minutes of stoppage time, the score remained unchanged. Galatasaray secured a 1–0 victory and earned its first three points in the group standings.

Galatasaray has solidified its position as the most title winning team in Turkish football history, a record breaking 24th time. Galatasaray has also completed 2023-24 season with a record breaking points won, collecting 102 points.

Galatasaray extend five players into new contracts

Galatasaray Spor Kulübü today announced the contract extensions with five players.The club has signed extensions with Lucas Torreira, Kaan Ayhan, Barış Alper Yılmaz, Abdülkerim Bardakcı and Fernando Muslera, officially presented in RAMS Park today by club's chairman Dursun Özbek.

All five players have committed their futures to Galatasaray, signing new contracts that will keep them at the club for the coming seasons.
